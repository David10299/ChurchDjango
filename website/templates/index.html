about us

A church family in Shenandoah centered on Christ

We’re a welcoming, bilingual (English/Spanish) congregation. We believe the Bible is God’s Word, salvation is by grace through faith in Jesus, and the Holy Spirit empowers us to live and serve.

Our heart is simple: worship Jesus, make disciples, and bless our town through practical love, prayer, and consistent presence.

Our Belief

Everything we believe begins and ends with Jesus.

We exist to glorify God by knowing Jesus and making Him known. He came to earth to offer himself for our sin and although He was killed, He defeated death by coming back to life when God raised Him. He ascended to heaven where He now sits at the right hand of God.

We believe in one God—Father, Son, and Holy Spirit. We believe Jesus Christ is the Son of God and the perfect expression of God’s love. Through His life, death, and resurrection, He paid the full price for our sins. Anyone who believes in Him receives forgiveness and eternal life, made new by the power of the Holy Spirit.
